    Deadlock is a MOBA where two teams of six battle each other to summon their respective patrons by destroying the enemy team's base. Each player chooses a hero with unique abilities and playstyles. For this project I will be showing the kits of three heroes and my thoughts on how fun they are to play, and how fun they are to play against.

    Basics of Deadlock

    The game is played on a map with three lanes, and a jungle in between. Each lane has one guardian, one walker , and two base guardians leading to the main base. Each base contains two shrines to the patron. Once both shrines are destroyed the patron can be harmed by the enemy team. The first team to destroy the enemy patron wins the game. Players earn souls by killing enemy heroes, minions, and base defenders. Souls are used to earn boons and buy items. Each hero has four abilities and starts the game with one of them, the other three are unlocked at boon 2, boon 4 and boon 6. All other boon levels increase the base power of the hero and grants an ability point that can be used to upgrade a hero's unlocked abilities. Each ability will have three level up bonuses that cost 1,2,and 5 ability points respectively, each giving different improvements for the ability. When arriving at various shops around the map players can use collected souls to buy items that give various stat boosts, passive effects, and active abilities.Souls are gained by killing enemy heroes, minions, base defenders, and neutral monsters in the jungle.

    Neutral Objectives

    In the jungle there are a few different resources that heroes can gain. Starting with the bridge buffs.
